Baked Feta Pasta

Description:

This is the story about another viral TikTok trend that I kinda sorted wanted to hate...but didn't. It's not that I didn't think the idea sounded delicious.
It's just that the whole thing seemed too simple. Obviously roasting a block of feta with bursting tomatoes is going to be amazing.
But is it good enough to break the internet?

Ingredients:

  • 2 pt. cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 1 shallot, quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1/2 c. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 (8-oz.) block feta
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 10 oz. pasta
  • Zest of 1 lemon (optional)
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Step:

    Preheat oven to 400°. In a large ovenproof skillet or medium baking dish, combine tomatoes, shallot, and garlic.
    Drizzle with about 1/4 cup oil and season with salt. Toss until fully coated.

  2. Step:

    Place feta in the center of the tomatoes and shallots and drizzle top with another 1/4 cup olive oil.
    Top with thyme sprigs. Bake until tomatoes are bursting and feta is melty, 30 to 35 minutes.

  3. Step:

    Meanwhile, in a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.
    Reserve ½ cup pasta water before draining.

  4. Step:

    Remove thyme from baking dish and add pasta.
    Stir until fully combined and season with more salt.
    If pasta seems dry, add a splash of reserved pasta water.
    Garnish with lemon zest and basil before serving.
